Welcome to Precious Nature

Precious Nature has a Magnificent structure which is considered as the Naturalistic Hotel in the city & entire southern Maharashtra, Precious Nature combines the warm hospitality of Kolhapur with the immaculate service of the Kolhapur brand. In just a short span of time, Precious Nature has become one of the most important landmarks of South Maharashtra.

Designed by the finest architects with 120 rooms, Precious Nature succeeds in mesmerizing the onlooker. The plush rooms, world-class amenities, ambient fine-dining restaurants and grand banquets make it the destination to be for the esteemed guests of the city. Kolhapur is home to the famous Mahalaxmi temple, situated in southern Maharashtra.
